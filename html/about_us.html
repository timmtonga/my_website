Our Mission

To design, develop and deliver infrastructure that enables learning from data, generation of knowledge, and translation into practice for the advancement of population health in the developing world.

Our Values

  1. Aim to Improve the experience of patients with the health system, hence we value the influence of technology on patient experience with a health system.
  2. We value systematic or holistic approaches over isolated technology interventions.
  3. We value evidence based decisions fuelled by credible data sources
  4. We embrace local settings and think of sociotechnical solutions within the local context
  5. We encourage continuous innovation with the view that each challenge is an opportunity to innovate.
  6. We promote scientific approaches to the whole lifecycle of implementing any technology intervention.
  7. We recognize also that in matters of truth or justice, there are no small or big problems hence we strive to treat everyone with respect and dignity regardless of who you are.
